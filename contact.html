Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How fast is delivery?

We deliver in under 30 minutes on average across the greater Cleveland area. Same-day delivery is available 7 days a week during business hours.

Is my first delivery really free?

Yes! Your first order gets free delivery with no minimum order size and no promo code needed. Just download the app, place your order, and enjoy.

Do you carry halal products?

Absolutely. We carry a wide selection of halal-certified products — clearly labeled in the app. We work with halal suppliers for our meat and prepared goods.

Can I place a wholesale or bulk order?

Yes! We work with restaurants, catering services, and community organizations. Email us at orders@clevelandafricanmart.com or fill out the contact form above.

Which areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to Cleveland, Cleveland Heights, Shaker Heights, Euclid, Parma, Lakewood, Garfield Heights, and surrounding areas. Check the app for your exact zip code.

How can I become a seller or supplier?

We're always looking for authentic African and international food suppliers. Reach out via the contact form, selecting "Become a Seller / Partner" as the subject.
