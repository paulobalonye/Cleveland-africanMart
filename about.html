About Cleveland African Mart

We are Cleveland's home for authentic African, Somali, and international groceries — founded by the community, built for the community.

"We started CFM because we knew what it felt like to miss the taste of home — and we believed Cleveland's African families deserved better."

Our Story

Cleveland African Mart was founded in 2022 by members of Cleveland's African diaspora community who were tired of driving hours to find authentic ingredients — or settling for substitutes that simply weren't the same.

We started with a simple idea: build a grocery service that truly understands what African families need. Not just the staple items — but the exact brands, the regional specialties, the fresh produce and dried fish that make a meal feel like home.

Today, CFM serves thousands of families across the greater Cleveland area. We carry 500+ products from West Africa, East Africa, Somalia, the Caribbean, Latin America, and beyond — all available through our mobile app with delivery in under 30 minutes.

We're not just a grocery service. We're a community hub — supporting local African events, partnering with community organizations, and helping Cleveland's diverse immigrant families stay connected to their culture through food.

Our Mission & Values

Everything we do is rooted in these four commitments to our community.

🌍

Authenticity First

We never substitute. Every product on CFM is the real thing — sourced from verified African and international suppliers who understand what authentic means.

🤝

Community-Driven

CFM was built by the community, for the community. A portion of every sale goes back to support local African cultural programs and events in Cleveland.

⚡

Speed & Reliability

We understand that fresh food can't wait. Our logistics network is built for speed — delivering across Cleveland in under 30 minutes, every day of the week.

🌱

Inclusive & Welcoming

From West African to Somali to Mexican to Caribbean — CFM is for everyone who loves diverse, authentic food. Every kitchen, every culture, welcome here.

Our Community Commitments

CFM gives back — here's how we invest in Cleveland's African community.

🎉 Cultural Events Sponsorship

We sponsor and support African cultural events, festivals, and celebrations across Cleveland — keeping our community's traditions alive and thriving.

🏫 Youth Programs

A portion of every CFM order supports youth literacy and cultural programs for African immigrant families in Cleveland's school system.

🛒 Supplier Partnerships

We prioritize partnering with African-owned food suppliers and import businesses, helping grow economic opportunity within our community.

🥘 Community Meals

We donate groceries to community meal programs and food banks serving Cleveland's African and immigrant families every month.
